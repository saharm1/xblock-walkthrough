Screen Reader Accessible Version of the Walkthrough

At the top of the screen are several tabs.

  • The Course tab is where all of the materials are kept, including videos, assignments, and readings. Any new updates from the course team can also be found on this page.
  • The Discussion tab is present in some but not all courses. This is where conversations with peers can be had.
  • The Progress tab is also present in some but not all courses. This is where you can keep track of your work in the course.

Each lesson is divided into a sequence of pages, which are accessed through a navigator ribbon that runs horizontally across the top of the screen.

You are currently viewing the first page of content in the Course tab.

The lessons are designed to be accessed from left to right. When you are done with this platform walkthrough, click either the Next button in the navigator ribbon or the right arrow.
